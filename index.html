Our story

Started with a cart and a craving.

LadyDawgs was born in 2020 when Angela Mann showed up to a Nashville market with a cart, a cooler full of the best dogs in town, and absolutely no plan to fail.

Five years later, that same cart shows up at farmers markets, festivals, and block parties across Middle Tennessee. The dogs got better. The lines got longer. The attitude stayed the same.

Every dog is loaded with house-made toppings, sourced from local vendors when possible, and assembled with the care of someone who genuinely gives a damn.
